Ocean Facts

Turtle

Turtles belong to one of the oldest reptile groups in the world – beating snakes, crocodiles and alligators!

Corals

Corals are in fact animals, not plants. They are naturally colorful because of algae, which lives inside of the coral, providing them with food.

Jellyfish

Jellyfish have no brain, heart, bones or eyes. They are made up of a smooth, bag-like body and tentacles armed with tiny, stinging cells.
